The goal of this project is to make a realistic stock trading game from scratch.

Wall Street game concept:

  • Upon sign-up, users receive $1 million of play money which can be used to trade stocks or play roulette/casino within the game.
  • All trades are based on actual market conditions. We track how much you've gained or lost on a particular transaction, pay dividends when applicable, etc.
  • Game leaderboards give you bragging rights for wealthiest, most active, most lucky at roulette/casino and other categories.

Here are some features which I'm considering developing:

  • Machine learning or automated trading.
  • Natural Language Processing to analyze the news related to the stock and output a sentiment analysis and also a corelation between them.
  • Set notification triggers (email, SMS) when stocks reach a specified price.
  • Ability to trade on margin and short sell. Also add Forex trading
  • More advanced graphs/charts (most likely using JavaScript.)
  • Historical stock data lookup.
  • Stats on the most active/best/worst-performing stocks of the day.
  • Links to articles you might be interested in, based on the stocks you look at or own.
  • Resources on how to learn more about the stock market and trading.
  • Possibility to sign in or create an account via Facebook or other social media.
  • Some decent design, maybe some ReactJs.
  • Let me know if you have other ideas!
In the meantime, check out the stock page.
